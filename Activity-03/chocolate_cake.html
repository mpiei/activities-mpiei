Soft and super moist one bowl chocolate cake with the fudgiest rich chocolate frosting! It's so easy to make and incredibly delicious.

Ingredients

  1. 1 cup (142g) all-purpose flour
  2. ⅓ cup (28g) cocoa powder
  3. ½ teaspoon baking powder
  4. ½ teaspoon baking soda
  5. ½ cup (110g) packed light brown sugar
  6. ¼ cup (50g) granulated sugar
  7. ¼ teaspoon salt
  8. 1 large egg
  9. ¼ cup (60ml) liquid oil (canola, olive, sunflower or vegetable)
  10. ⅓ cup (80ml) full fat sour cream
  11. ½ teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  12. ½ cup (120ml) hot coffee or hot water

Cake Recipe

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Line an 8x8 or 9x9-inch square metal pan with parchment paper.
  2. In a large bowl, sift together flour, cocoa, baking powder, and baking soda. Whisk in sugar and salt.
  3. In another bowl, whisk egg, oil, sour cream, and vanilla until smooth. Add to dry ingredients and mix with a hand mixer on medium-low. Gradually mix in hot coffee until batter is smooth.
  4. Spread batter in pan. Bake 20–25 minutes until springy and a skewer comes out clean. Cool in pan 15 minutes, then invert onto a rack to cool completely.
  5. For frosting: Gently heat cream, honey/corn syrup, butter, salt, and chopped dark chocolate in a saucepan. Stir until smooth and glossy.
  6. Transfer to a bowl. Stir in sour cream. Chill 15–20 minutes until pudding-like. Whisk briefly to lighten before using. Do not over-whip.
